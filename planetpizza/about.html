About Us

OUR STORY

Pizza Planet began in 2013 when we borrowed $4,000 and set up shop in a failed pizza place in Northeast Minneapolis, just a hop skip and a jump from Interstate 35. With that modest amount of money, we bought some flour, cheese, imported spices and other high-quality ingredients and began promoting our pizzas locally to neighborhood groups having benefits or gatherings. As time went on, we learned how to reach people through strong direct mail campaigns and online advertising. Today our established location draws a significant lunch and happy hour crowd due to our close proximity to the downtown business district. We also bring in many local families who love our pizza as much as they love to support local business....
